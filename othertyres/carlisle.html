Carlisle Trac Chief®

Carlisle Tyres

The Carlisle Trac Chief® is a heavy-duty tyre designed for industrial and construction equipment, offering exceptional durability, traction, and performance. It is suitable for skid steers, compact tractors, aerial work platforms, and other industrial machinery operating in demanding conditions.

Features

1. Durable Construction: Built with a robust, heavy-duty carcass to withstand the toughest industrial and construction environments. High resistance to cuts, punctures, and abrasions.

2. Excellent Traction: Deep, Angled Lugs: Provide superior grip and traction on loose, muddy, and uneven terrains. Designed to perform reliably in off-road and heavy-duty applications.

3. High Load Capacity: Engineered to support heavy equipment and ensure stability during operation. Ideal for lifting, hauling, and other high-load industrial tasks.

4. Versatile Performance: Suitable for various surfaces, including dirt, gravel, mud, and concrete. Performs well in challenging conditions, including wet and slippery surfaces.

5. Long Tread Life: Tough tread compound ensures slow and even wear, providing extended tyre life and reduced downtime.

Description

The Carlisle Trac Chief® is a heavy-duty industrial tyre designed for skid steers, compact tractors, and construction equipment. It features a deep-lug tread for superior traction on varied terrains, a robust carcass for durability, and high load capacity for demanding tasks, ensuring long service life and reliable performance in tough conditions.
