BKT W 207 Front/Rear

BKT Tyres

The BKT W 207 is a versatile All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV) tyre designed for exceptional performance on a variety of terrains. Its robust construction and durable design make it an excellent choice for both front and rear fitment on ATVs

Features

1. Durable Construction: 6-Ply Rating: Offers enhanced durability and puncture resistance, ideal for rugged and off-road applications.

2. Versatile Performance: Suitable for multiple terrains, including soft, intermediate, and hard surfaces. Performs well in off-road conditions like mud, dirt, and loose gravel.

3. Aggressive Tread Design: Deep, angled lugs ensure excellent traction and grip for improved control on challenging terrains. Wide spacing between lugs provides efficient self-cleaning to maintain traction.

4. Balanced Design: Can be used as both a front and rear tyre, making it highly versatile for ATV applications. Optimized tread pattern for stability and smooth performance.

5. High Load Capacity: Designed to handle heavy loads, making it suitable for utility and recreational ATV use.

Description

The BKT W 207 is an all terrain, all season tire manufactured for ATVs and UTVs. The tire’s aggressive directional pattern with the detailed tread elements increases the soft, loose and uneven terrain surface traction. The tread’s high void ratio and optimal lug placement avoids stone retention and maintains a clean footprint at all times. The W 207 is easily controlled with the constant terrain surface contact of the ideal tread design.
