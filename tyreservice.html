Tyre Services

Inspect the Tyre

Begin by thoroughly inspecting the tyre for visible damage.
•Check for punctures in the tread area or sidewall.
•Look for any cracks, bulges, or deep cuts that could be unsafe.
•Examine the valve stem to ensure it’s intact and not leaking air.
•Inspect the rim for any signs of bending, corrosion, or cracks.
•Measure the tyre's tread depth to determine if it needs replacement.
•Ensure the tyre is free from any debris lodged in the tread.
•Verify the tyre’s pressure is appropriate before removal.
•Make sure the wheel is properly aligned with the vehicle.

•Use a Use a car jack to lift the vehicle off the ground.

•Fully Fully remove the nuts once the tyre is off the ground.
•RemoveRemove the wheel and set it aside on a stable surface.
•EnsureEnsure the vehicle remains stable while the tyre is off.
