Perfinza CLX1

MRF Winter Tyres

The MRF Perfinza CLX1 is a premium winter tyre designed for SUVs and passenger vehicles, offering reliable performance and safety in challenging winter conditions, including snow, slush, and wet roads.

Features

1.Enhanced Winter Traction Special winter tread compound ensures flexibility in low temperatures for better grip on icy and snowy roads. Optimized tread design with multiple sipes improves braking and cornering stability in winter conditions.

2.Reliable Wet Performance Wide circumferential grooves and lateral channels effectively disperse water and slush, reducing hydroplaning risks. High-density tread blocks ensure superior traction on wet surfaces.

3.Superior Wet Grip High-silica rubber compound ensures excellent braking and cornering on wet roads. Optimized groove design reduces hydroplaning risks by efficiently evacuating water.

4.Durability and Longevity Even tread wear pattern and robust construction ensure a longer tyre lifespan. Resistant to harsh winter road conditions, including salt and debris.

5.Certified Winter Safety 3PMSF (Three-Peak Mountain Snowflake) certified for severe snow performance. Designed to minimize road noise and vibrations for a smooth, quiet ride.

Description

The Michelin Pilot Alpin 5 is engineered for drivers seeking optimal control and confidence in winter driving conditions. Its blend of advanced technology and winter-specific design ensures a safe and enjoyable driving experience, even in challenging weather.
